# 💫 About Me:
I'm Daniel Garzón, though online I'm known as Z3RO. A true Cordoban, I've been passionate about technology for as long as I can remember. My story with coding began at age nine, entirely self-taught. What started as pure childhood curiosity has become my greatest competitive advantage: an innate ability to understand any programming logic.

Currently, I work on the front line of technical operations for Telefónica, managing critical infrastructure by configuring Cisco, Fortinet, Teldat, and Edge environments. I'm responsible for ensuring that networks and security function flawlessly when there's no room for error.

However, my true calling is development. My aspiration is to join a team where the challenge is software; I don't care about the language, I care about the solution. If there's documentation, I can master it. Outside of servers and code, you'll find me soaking up the sun of my homeland: out on the road, taking walks, or escaping to the beach to recharge.
